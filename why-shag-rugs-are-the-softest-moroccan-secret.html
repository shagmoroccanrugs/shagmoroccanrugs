What makes Moroccan shag rugs different from other types of rugs?

Moroccan shag rugs are unique due to their thick, soft pile and intricate designs that reflect the rich cultural heritage of Morocco. They often feature bold geometric patterns and are handcrafted using traditional techniques.

Are shag rugs suitable for high-traffic areas in my home?

While shag rugs are soft and luxurious, they may not be the best choice for high-traffic areas as they can show wear more quickly than low-pile rugs. However, choosing a durable material and placing them in lower-traffic spaces can help maintain their beauty.

How do I clean and maintain my Moroccan shag rug?

To clean a Moroccan shag rug, regularly vacuum it on a low setting to remove dirt and debris. For deeper cleaning, use a professional rug cleaner or follow specific care instructions based on the material of your rug. Avoid excessive moisture to prevent damage.
