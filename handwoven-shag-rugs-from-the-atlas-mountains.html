Cleaning and Maintenance Tips

Proper care is essential for preserving the beauty and longevity of Handwoven Shag Rugs from the Atlas Mountains. Utilizing premium-grade wool, these rugs feature intricate designs inspired by centuries-old craft techniques. To maintain their texture and appearance, regular vacuuming on a flat surface is advisable to remove dust and debris. If a spill occurs, promptly tackle it by blotting the area with a clean cloth, avoiding harsh chemicals that may compromise the integrity of the rug. For deeper cleaning, consider professional services that specialize in artisanal weaving techniques to ensure that the rug reflects its vintage charm.

For maintenance, air-drying outdoors is an effective method for revitalizing Handwoven Shag Rugs from the Atlas Mountains. Avoid exposing them to direct sunlight for extended periods to prevent fading. Rugs should be rolled rather than folded when stored to maintain their shape, and using rug weft substitution can help sustain their quality over time. As decorative floor coverings, these vintage pieces add depth to the interior design world, making it crucial to treat them with care for lasting enjoyment. Covering the rugs when not in use can also protect them from environmental factors that may lead to deterioration.
